My research grant "[CRII: CSR: Co-Simulation-Based Optimization of Container Orchestration Platforms](https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/show-award?AWD_ID=2451392)" was funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF) with an amount of $150,000. The project develops Cosimo, a co-simulation framework that integrates real container orchestration platforms with simulated applications to optimize resource management in systems like Kubernetes through multi-objective optimization.
