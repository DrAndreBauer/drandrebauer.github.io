Two of our research papers have been accepted for presentation at the 16th Symposium on Software Performance ([SSP](https://www.performance-symposium.org)):
* "Towards Intelligent Performance Data Analytics With Graph Databases" – We explore the use of graph databases for intelligent performance data analytics.
* "A Case Study on the Value of Simulated and Synthetic Microservice Applications for Performance Model Training" – We investigate how simulated and synthetic microservice applications can improve performance model training.
