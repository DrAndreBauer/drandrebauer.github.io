Two of our research papers have been accepted for presentation at the Companion of the 21st IEEE International Conference on e-Science ([eScience](https://www.escience-conference.org/2025/)):
* "EvoAgents: Generative AI for Rapid Architecture Search in Cell Nuclei Detection" – We leverage generative AI to automate neural architecture search for cell nuclei detection tasks.
* "Finding the Sweet Spot: Speed vs. Fidelity in Encrypted LLMs via Proxy Simulation" – We explore the trade-off between speed and fidelity when running large language models under encryption using proxy simulation.
