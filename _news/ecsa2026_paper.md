Our paper, "graphobs: Composable Graph and Observability Queries for Microservice Systems", has been accepted at the 20th European Conference on Software Architecture ([ECSA 2026](https://conf.researchr.org/home/ecsa-2026)) in Bolzano, Italy. We introduce a query approach that composes graph and observability queries to support exploration and analysis of microservice systems.
