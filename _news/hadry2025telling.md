Our research paper, "[Telling Fortunes? Evaluation of Traffic Forecasting Models using Traffic and Context Features](https://doi.org/10.1007/s10489-025-06565-4)", has been accepted for publication in Applied Intelligence. We evaluate traffic forecasting models by incorporating both traffic and contextual features, shedding light on how context information impacts prediction quality.
