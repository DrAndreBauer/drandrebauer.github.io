Our research paper, "[Challenges in the Evaluation of Earth Observation Products: Accuracy Assessment Case Study Using Convolutional Neural Networks](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2352938524002842)", has been accepted for publication in Remote Sensing Applications: Society and Environment. We present an accuracy assessment case study using CNNs, highlighting the challenges in evaluating Earth observation products.
