Our research paper, "An Empirical Study on Transient Phases of Microservice Applications", has been accepted for presentation at the 33rd International Symposium on Modeling, Analysis and Simulation of Computer and Telecommunication Systems ([MASCOTS](https://mascots25.iitis.pl/)). We empirically study the transient phases of microservice applications, providing insights into their warm-up behavior and its impact on performance evaluation.
